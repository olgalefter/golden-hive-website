Our Story

Golden Hive is a family owned apiary founded and run by Mihail and Maria Daschevici. Mihail’s journey into the magical world of bees and honey was sparked by an elderly beekeeper, Ilie Caus, who believed in beekeeping as a way of life. Mihail’s initial goal was to master the craft of beekeeping and maintain a few hives enough to produce honey for his family. He’s been maintaining and promoting a healthy lifestyle since his 20’s and wanted to offer his little girls a natural and nutrient substitute for sugar.

He started small but the more he learned his passion for for bees, beekeeping and honey grew and this is how the Golden Hive apiary was born. His wife Maria shares Mihail’s passion and is a key supporter in their small family business dedicated to producing only raw, unfiltered honey and natural bee products. Moldova has an excellent climate for beekeeping with rich valleys and forests which allows the Daschevici family to transport the beehives between harvesting times to blooming plants and yield honey and bee products just as nature intended.

Together they strive to live as bees live: symbiotically with nature and in a manner that contributes positively to the world around us.
