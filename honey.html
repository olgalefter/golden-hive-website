Honey

For millennia, honey was only considered a sweetener, whereas today we know that it contains many other substances that are beneficial for people. It is an excellent nutrient and calmative. Owing to its antibacterial and antioxidant properties, it is widely used in folk medicine.

We proudly produce 100% pure acacia, linden and sunflower honey.

Acacia Honey

Color & Taste

Light golden color with an incredible clarity. Mild taste containing hints of vanilla.

Composition

Made from acacia flower and spring blossoms nectar.

Properties

Considered the lightest tasting honey in the world. The natural sugar profile of acacia honey resists crystallization.

Health Benefits

It has a very low sucrose content and a high fructose level, so it is the best choice for diabetics. Due to its therapeutic qualities it cleanses the liver and conditions the intestines. It is beneficial for the respiratory system due to antibacterial properties.

Linden Honey

Color & Taste

With a light amber color linden honey has a fresh woody with a hint of mint, balsamic and camphor aroma. Linden honey is sweet, sometimes with a touch of bitterness. After it you can always feel persistent aftertaste and light astringency.

Composition

Made from linden flower nectar.

Properties

About 3 months after harvesting the linden honey it begins to crystallize: it thickens and loses its transparency. But white honey does not lose its useful properties and taste.

Health Benefits

Due to its high anti-bacterial properties it’s used in remedies for cold and gastrointestinal tract diseases. Because it contains almost a full range of vitamins, minerals, essential oils and trace elements it’s frequently used in cosmetology. It also has a mild sedative effect, which helps to calm the mind and body.
